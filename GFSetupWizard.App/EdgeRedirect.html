Microsoft Edge Profile Sync Setup

You need to manually navigate to the Edge sync settings page. This page will guide you through the process.

Method 1: Direct Navigation

  1. Copy this URL: edge://settings/profiles/sync
  2. Paste it into the address bar at the top of this Edge window
  3. Press Enter

Method 2: Menu Navigation

  1. Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner of Edge
  2. Click on "Manage profile settings" or the gear icon
  3. In the left sidebar, click on "Sync"

Setting Up Work Account Sync

  1. On the sync settings page, sign in with your GlobalFoundries work account if prompted
  2. When asked to choose an account type, select "Work or school account"
  3. Complete the authentication process with your company credentials
  4. On the sync settings page, ensure all sync options are enabled
  5. If you see a message about "Sync across your signed-in devices", click "Turn on sync"

Recommended Sync Settings

  • Favorites
  • Settings
  • Form fill data
  • Passwords
  • History
  • Open tabs
  • Extensions
  • Collections

Benefits of Syncing

  • Access your work bookmarks on all devices
  • Seamlessly continue browsing across devices
  • Keep your work browsing separate from personal
  • Secure access to company resources
  • Consistent browsing experience

Note: If you're having trouble accessing the sync settings, you may need to restart Edge or check with your IT department for any specific configuration requirements.

Once you've completed the sync setup, you can close this window and return to the GF Setup Wizard.
